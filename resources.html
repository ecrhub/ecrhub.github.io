🌍 Global Climate Data Resources

🌍 Global Reanalysis Datasets

These datasets provide a consistent reconstruction of past climate states using models and observations.

  • ERA5 (ECMWF Reanalysis v5) – Provided by Copernicus; hourly data from 1950 onward
  • NCEP/NCAR Reanalysis – Global reanalysis since 1948
  • JRA-55 (Japanese Reanalysis) – Japanese Meteorological Agency, from 1958
  • MERRA-2 – NASA reanalysis from 1980 onward

🌀 Seasonal and Subseasonal Forecast Data

  • NMME – Multi-model seasonal forecast system
  • Copernicus Seasonal Forecasts (C3S) – ECMWF-led multi-model seasonal forecasts

☁️ Climate Observations and Satellite Datasets

  • IMD Gridded Rainfall Data (India) – High-resolution gridded datasets for India
  • NOAA Climate Data Online (CDO) – Access to U.S. and global station data
  • NASA Giovanni – Interactive access to satellite and model data
